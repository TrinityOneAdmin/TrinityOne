Safeguarding

Protection anchored in your leaders

The young people in your care are guarded by the same trust you extend in person. Your leaders mark who is a minor and who is an approved adult, and messages to a minor are gated through their guardians.

  • Your leaders decide who is a minor and who is approved
  • Messages to a minor pass through their guardians
For stewards: the safeguarding lists are owner-only, and your own country's background-check process fits — nothing is tied to one nation's scheme.
