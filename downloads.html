Good to know

Whichever way suits you

Works on any device

On an iPhone, a computer, or anything with a web browser, just open it online — there is nothing to install.

Android gets the full app

On an Android phone you can install the app itself for the smoothest experience, and to keep using it even when you are offline.

A note when installing

Because you are installing the app directly, Android may show a warning — that is normal. Just tap through to allow it, and you are set.
