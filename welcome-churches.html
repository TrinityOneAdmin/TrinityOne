Run your own relay

Your church runs on TrinityOne’s shared relays out of the box — nothing to set up. Want a copy of every event on hardware you control? Order a managed relay in a click, or run the free TrinityOne Relay app on any Mac, Windows, or Linux machine. The Steward console just points your church at it — your key signs each change, and never leaves your device.

  • Managed by us, or the desktop Relay app — your call
  • Relay app runs on Mac, Windows or Linux — no command line
  • Always falls back to shared relays
Run it yourself — one line, then finish in your browser

Paste this on any Debian/Ubuntu/Raspberry Pi box (a Pi, mini-PC, old laptop or VPS). It installs the relay as a boot service, then prints a dashboard link — open it and click Connect → Make it public for a secure public address. No GitHub account, and no more terminal after this line.

Safe & well-run

A community you can keep safe

Because the church knows its people, protection is anchored in your leaders — not a meaningless tick-box. These tools support your safeguarding policy and background checks; they don't replace them.

Safeguarding for under-18s

Mark who's a child and which adults are cleared for youth. Children see only child-safe groups, and a private message between a child and an adult is blocked unless that adult is cleared — enforced on your relay, not just the app.

Parent-linked accounts

A parent sets up and holds their child's account from their own phone, and can always reach their own child. You confirm every link, so no one can attach themselves to a child who isn't theirs.

Choose how people join

Keep it open — anyone with your code or QR joins instantly — or switch on approval, and new people wait in a simple requests list until a steward lets them in.

Shape your space

Turn whole features on or off for your congregation, pin an announcement to the top of a group, and remove a message or block a member when you need to.

Run your church

The admin, handled

The behind-the-scenes work a church actually runs on — sharing the load, giving records & Gift Aid, rooms and rotas — in the same place your people already are. Self-custodial, so no company holds your data.

Share the load with stewards

Don't run it alone. Invite a trusted helper as a steward to help manage the church — and here's the careful part: each steward acts under their own key, never yours. Your church key is never shared, and you can add or remove a steward at any time.

Giving records & Gift Aid Optional

An optional treasurer's ledger: keep Gift-Aid-ready donor records, categorise funds, and import a bank statement — it matches deposits to donors — then export the HMRC schedule. Every record is encrypted to your church key. (Record-keeping only; in-app Lightning giving is on the roadmap.)

Shared room calendar

Book the hall, rooms and spaces in one place — with a double-booking check so the same room is never promised twice. See what's on, when, at a glance.

Serving & rotas

Build teams and rotas, save serving pods that rotate week to week, and send "Can you serve?" — members get a notification on their phone and reply in a tap.
